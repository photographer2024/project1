Historical Places

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Great Pyramid of Giza

The Great Pyramid of Giza is the oldest and largest of the three pyramids in the Giza Necropolis, Egypt. It was built as a tomb for Pharaoh Khufu.

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is an Inca citadel located in Peru. It was built in the 15th century and abandoned before the arrival of the Spanish conquistadors.
